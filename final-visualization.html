Below is our final visualization. It features a bar chart which depicts aggregated counts of participation or interest in various alumni events. The events can be toggled from those already conducted in the past to those possibly planned for the future through the use of the two accordingly named buttons, which can be found above the bar chart. To the right of the bar chart is a pie chart which reflects the proportion of alumni who are still connected to their respective MPF networks. This visualization updates on its own when users mouse-over a bar in the bar chart, changing to reflect responses specific to users who participated or expressed interest in the event tied to that specific bar.
