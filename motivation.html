Our project is to analyze and provide visualizations for data that would help answer the following questions: In what ways are Alums engaging in the MPF experience? What are the opportunities for Alums to reconnect with the organization? This is a complex topic, because it forces considerations of the past, present, and even the future, all to be extrapolated from a limited data sample. We hope to effectively address these questions in such a way that will both lead to understanding of alumni involvement in the past, and how it can be used to project and adapt to the future.

At first glance, it seems the most important data with respect to our task are responses “How have you engaged with MPF in the past year?”. While simple, simply analyzing such responses seems like a straightforward way to gain easy insight into the present state of alumni involvement. However, considering how drastically circumstances have changed since early 2020, it seemed unreasonable to prioritize this as though nothing had changed. Rather, our analysis will focus largely on the availability of future opportunities and the desire of alumni to partake in those opportunities.
